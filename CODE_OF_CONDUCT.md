# Code of Conduct

We want NSLAVE to be a welcoming, constructive community.

## Be respectful
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, communicate clearly.

## Keep it safe
- No doxxing or sharing private information.
- No scam links, phishing, or impersonation.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or ban users who violate these rules.
